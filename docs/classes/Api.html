burySarcophagus

  • burySarcophagus(sarcoId, options?): Promise<TransactionResponse>

  • Invalidates the Sarcophagus by setting a MaxUint32 resurrection time. Once this is done, the sarcophagus can never be resurrected. Cursed archaeologists are freed from their curse and their locked bonds are returned to them.
